About this Tracker

Interact with the timeline or map to explore India's clean energy transition. This interface visualizes green power capacity (solar, wind, small hydro, biomass/waste) and compares it with conventional grid sources (coal, lignite, gas, diesel, nuclear) where applicable.

Sources: MNRE, Renewable Energy Statistics 2024-25. Conventional: CEA/National Power Portal state installed capacity reports (as of 31.03.2025). Share trend: CEA Growth of Electricity Sector 2025; 2025-26 uses MoP capacity up to Jan 31, 2026. Sources Over Time & the story tour use the same report's year-wise renewable (RES) figures — Table 2.1 for all-India and Chapters 11–15 (Tables 11.2.1–15.2.1) for the Top 5 states; these RES categories count small hydro and exclude large hydro. Boundaries: INDIAN-SHAPEFILES (MIT), shown as per the Government of India / Survey of India political map. Story-tour photos: real images of Indian solar parks & wind farms from Wikimedia Commons (Bhadla, Mandvi/Kutch, Muppandal, Pavagada) under CC BY / CC BY-SA / ESA Copernicus licenses — see image credits.
